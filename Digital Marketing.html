Digital Marketing

Digital marketing is essential for businesses to reach their target audience online. It encompasses a range of strategies and tools to enhance brand visibility, drive website traffic, and convert leads into customers.

Core Skills Required

  • SEO (Search Engine Optimization): Driving organic traffic through search engines
  • Social Media Marketing: Creating and managing campaigns on platforms like Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn
  • Email Marketing: Crafting effective email campaigns
  • Content Marketing: Developing engaging and informative content
  • Analytics and Reporting: Using tools like Google Analytics to measure performance
  • Paid Advertising: Running PPC campaigns on Google Ads and social platforms

Popular Digital Marketing Projects

  • Search engine optimization for websites
  • Social media account setup and management
  • Content marketing strategies and execution
  • Email campaign creation and automation
  • Google Ads and Facebook Ads campaigns
  • Analytics reports and strategy recommendations
Minimum Stipend Expectations:
  • SEO Specialists: 160Rs. - 400Rs. per hour
  • Social Media Marketers: 120Rs. - 320Rs. per hour
  • Content Marketers: 200Rs. - 480Rs. per hour
  • PPC Experts: 240Rs. - 560Rs. per hour

Project rates vary depending on the scope but can range from $300 for basic campaigns to $15,000+ for large-scale marketing strategies.
